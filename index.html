About the Author

Hi, everyone! My name is Kaylee, and I like to read, write, hike, and code (in fact, I created this page using Github, a software development program that requires you to code to make your website). I am a twelve-year old Lord of the Rings fanatic, animal lover, and young climate change activist. Anyone a Lord of the Rings fan here? (Believe me, I'm dedicated Tolkien lover. I named my dog Frodo.) I like watching Intelligence Squared videos, participating in Model Congress debates, and forming theses for my argumentative papers. Because I've been interested in the news lately, I have decided to make a news website to voice my thoughts and opinions on such topics.

Frodo, Best Dog Ever!

My goal for this website is to inform, entertain, and educate people of all ages from around the world. I will not only cover national/world events, but also local news. Want to know what's going on in your city or state? Check The Daily Kaylee to find out! I will also post links to various websites in which I thought were useful in writing my post. Hope you enjoy!

War with a Jaguar is a short story that is about a young giant otter, Patch, who is petrified of jaguars. When Mamba, the fiercest jaguar in the Pantanal, terrorizes the main dens, giant otters are in absolute distress. Will Patch face her fears by fighting and risk being killed by Mamba? Read this story to find out, as well as to learn new facts about what else threatens giant otters.
