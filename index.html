About the Author

Hi, everyone! My name is Kaylee, and I like to read, write, hike, and code. I like watching Intelligence Squared videos, participating in Model Congress debates, and forming theses for my argumentative papers. I have decided to create the Daily Kaylee to voice my thoughts and opinions on such topics.

My pup, Frodo, likes to sit on my lap while I type up my stories. Best dog ever!

War with a Jaguar

War with a Jaguar is a short story that is about a young giant otter, Patch, who is petrified of jaguars. When Mamba, the fiercest jaguar in the Pantanal, terrorizes the main dens, giant otters are in absolute distress. Will Patch face her fears by fighting and risk being killed by Mamba? Read this story to find out, as well as to learn new facts about what else threatens giant otters.
