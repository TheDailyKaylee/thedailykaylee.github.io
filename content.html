Mythical Creatures in Isle Andia (ISENA)

Lenas - Multicolored squirrels

Sirnanas - Leafy green insects with blue and yellow colored wings. Poisonous bite and stinger, but very intelligent.

Normal Phoenix - Regular brightly colored orange bird with minor healing powers. Common, so not worth THAT much. Found everywhere.

Lightning Phoenix - Reddish orange, like the normal phoenix, but with lightning bolts all around it. Has the power of lightning, healing only burns. Rare in tropical places, cities, sky realms, and over populated areas. Sometimes expensive. Usually found in Devina.

Rain Phoenix - Violet, blue, and silver. Has the power of healing most minor and major wounds, unlike other Phoenixes and can blast enemies with a sheet of water. The most intelligent of all phoenixes, they can also do major changes in weather. Mostly found in the deepest parts of Dihersan, and well hidden. Very rare and expensive!

Wind Phoenixes - Can heal most lightning and ice wounds and quite pretty with white, silver and light celestial blue colors. Moderately rare and expensive, but if you get a good one, they can win a lot of Phoenix shows!

Chamdragons- Small dragons (around the size of your palm) that change color.

Element dragons - Fire (Red, orange, gold color), Earth (bronze, green, gold color), Water (purple, silver, blue color), Air (white, silver, gold color).

Rainbow leopards - Red, yellow, green, and blue leopards that you can ride on. They are very friendly and good at attacking enemies.

Hypnas - Flying balls of fur that can attack and bite with teeth.

Desert lizards - Poisonous descendants of dragons with stingers. Some good, some bad.

Quiten - Flying spotted kittens

Winged Creatures - Non magical animals (such as cats, rats, lions...etc.) with white wings attached to them.

Griffin - Has the head/wings of an eagle and the body of a lion. Can support up to three people (at full length) when flying.

Sphinx - Has the body of a lion, and the head of a woman.

Fae - Fairies.

Unisi - Unicorns, Pegasi.

Gem Dragons - Fancy dragons found in the Dwarf Region. Strong, but they don’t have many powers.

Tellae - Blue alien creatures that look like lizards. Some rare and expensive, but they have no powers except for making people drowsy.

Ghost Fish - Dangerous dark fish that can take your soul away if looked in the eyes.

Dragonites - Smaller versions of dragons that basically have the same powers, but are sometimes more powerful because of their quick speed… Careful of them, though, as adults tend to be nippy. They are found in the heart of Pine’s Crest, Crystal Deep.

Lands in Isle Andia (ISENA)

Theran Woods - Looks like a normal forest except with glowing colorful mushrooms.

Cave of Dar - The giant’s Cave at the edge of Theran Woods to the East.

Dihersan - An island in the Jayn Ocean. Beautiful sandy beaches and green tropical forests.

Rhea’s Tower - Rhea’s Tower is in the center of Dihersan. Built by stone and ivy, it looms 100 feet tall.

Pine’s Crest - An Island off Dihersan, filled with dragons.

Crystal Deep - The heart of Pine’s Crest, full of Dragonites. A well hidden clearing.

The Thrush Of Dell - A clearing west of the Theran Woods. Purple bushes and grass are everywhere, and rainbow colored trees. A nice resting place.

Devina - A desert filled with poisonous monsters.

Dragon’s Firefly - In the desert lands of Devina, there is a small oasis where there is a bar. Drunken pirates go there to sell dragons.

Bree - A land that is mostly prairie.

Town Of Ivyl - A cobblestone town in Bree. Many shops are there.

Central Isle Andia - A forest land with many clearings. Rich and luxurious, good trading center.

Crystalline Castle - A beautiful turquoise diamond colored castle in Central Isle Andia. King Lewis and Queen Rey live there.

The Mountain of Thor - A crystal mountain in Central Isle Andia.

Vival Market - A lively market in Central Isle Andia.

Fair Lands - West Of Crystalline Castle, where the pixies and fairies live. Much lush greenery and different kinds of mushrooms.

Fae Palace - A fairy palace in the Fair Lands made out of marble.

Sterling - A small town near the Sea of Nell

Snowice - An icy region filled with Yetis and Snow Unisi

West Snowice (Dwarf Region) - Many rich mines are found here.

Snowdell - East of Snowice and more populated with many taverns and cottages.

Lakedell - The huge elven region in West Isle Andia

Runien - The grand castle of Lake dell.

Forgest - The volcano west of Devina.

Skyenel - The cloud city in the sky.

Merielieina - The castle in Skyenel.

Sky Trading Center - Trading center right next to Merielieina.

The Woods Of Blue Hope - Deep woods, gloomy at night, cheerful at day. The sunlight turns blue here.

Merlin’s Fortress - In the edge of The Woods Of Blue Hope. A tall, blue fortress/castle where many people go there for training.

Morgan (le Fay)’s Great Mist Of Illusions - 30 miles West of Merlin’s Fortress. Here, the sunlight turns an ultraviolet purple. An Evil Sorceress lives hiding in this territory, but no one knows where she is.

Chance - At the edge of the world, Chance is a place where the most unexpected things happen. No one rules Chance.

Mini World - The other side of the planet, where storybook characters live. A very small territory. Another side to the world of Isle Andia…

The Vines Of Doom - Where Maleficent lives. A dark black and purple castle with green mist surrounding it.

Icy Terror - The region where the Snow Queen lives. A tall castle made out of ice. Appears to be light blue, and the tops are very pointy.

The Poison Apple Woods - Where the Evil Queen lives. Has dark, thick woods.

Mirroren - The castle in Poison Apple Woods. The castle is very similar to the rest of the woods.

The River Of Oddities - In the Poison Apple Woods, there is a river of Oddities where if you fall in, you can never come out.

Rapunzel Tower - Where Rapunzel used to live.

The Little Farmland (Giant’s Vine) - Where Goldilocks, Little Red Riding Hood, Jack and the Beanstalk, Goldilocks, Little Bo Peep...etc lives. Very sunny farm land with bright forests/woods.

The Charming Kingdoms - Where all the princesses and princes live.

The Lands Of Forgotten Mysterious Objects - Where other characters live. A very large territory that includes Wonderland, and Oz.

Disputed Territory - Where forgotten enemies live.

Sombrien - A dreamlike portal planet where the Wise Elves live.

Fruits, Drinks, and Other Foods

(Other human foods are found on this planet as well, such as apples.)

Pomnns - The common fruit that can be found in the Theran Woods. Pomnns’s, unlike Frondse are sour, small, and red.

Frondse - A medium sized fruit shaped like a small pumpkin. Its color varies from dark blue to light green, and is very popular in Bree because of its sweetness. Nickname - Fair bluebells.

Alonae - Purplish sweet seaweed that tastes like vanilla. Used for frosting most commonly.

Phlorav - Yellow berry pastries wrapped in fresh dew leaves. Restores energy.

Aerien - Celestial blue and white cupcakes.

...etc. Currently working on more foods.
