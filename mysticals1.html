Mysticals Role Play: Save the Mythical Creatures!

In this role play game, the adventure calls for you (a member of the Mythical Creature Rescue Committee) to go to the coast of Lyrae. The creatures there (mostly dragons and unicorns) are threatened by Lyddia, the famous sorceress, and need to evacuate before she tries to manipulate their powers...
